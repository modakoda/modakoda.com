§ Intelligence

AI that earns its place in your product.

LLM Integration

We embed language models into existing products — not as chatbots bolted onto the side, but as features that understand context and deliver real outcomes. RAG pipelines, function calling, and fine-tuning where generic models fall short.

Intelligent Automation

Move beyond rule-based workflows. We build systems that read documents, classify requests, and make decisions — then hand off to humans only when judgment is genuinely required. Measurable time savings from day one.

AI Infrastructure

Model serving, prompt versioning, observability, and cost controls. We run the plumbing so your team can experiment safely and ship faster without waking up to surprise API bills.

Discovery & Roadmap

Not every problem needs a model. We audit your workflows, identify high-confidence AI opportunities, and build a roadmap ranked by feasibility and return. No solutionism — just clear thinking.
